Welcome to The Happy Family! 👋

🧹 Chore Tracker (Kid's App)

• Your kid opens the Chore Tracker app and checks off chores they complete

• You get a Telegram message instantly when a chore is done

• Reply yes to approve or no to reject directly in Telegram

• Approved chores add money to the balance automatically


⚡ Super Panel (Your App)

• Overview — see weekly points and activate the Super Button at end of week

• Approvals — approve/reject chores and kung fu attendance

• Chores — add, remove and manage chores and their pay amounts

• 💰 Money — track earnings, deduct money, mark as paid

• PC — shut down PC, lock screen, view screen live, manage screen time


📊 Prediction (in Chore Tracker)

• Toggle which chores your kid plans to complete this month

• See predicted monthly and yearly paycheck instantly

• Missed chores automatically reduce the prediction


🤖 Telegram Bot Commands

• yes — approve a chore ✅

• no — reject a chore ❌

• excused — excuse a kung fu absence

• not excused — mark as unexcused (+100 points)

• pending — see all pending approvals

• balance — check money owed


⚡ Points & Super Button

• Missed chore (not approved) = +50 points

• Missed kung fu (not excused) = +100 points

• 100 points = 1 hour of screen time removed per day next week

• Max 6 hours/day screen time

• Press Super Button at end of week to apply penalties

• Points reset every week automatically
